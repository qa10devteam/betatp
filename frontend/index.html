Edge = our model probability minus the bookmaker's implied probability. Formula: edge = model_prob - (1 / pinnacle_odds). A positive edge means we believe the market is mispricing the outcome. We only publish picks with edge above 5%.
Area Under the ROC Curve. An AUC of 0.935 means in 93.5% of comparisons, the model correctly ranks the true outcome above the incorrect one. A random model scores 0.5; a perfect model scores 1.0. Our models range 0.833 to 0.935, well above market efficiency.
Predictions are bookmaker-agnostic. We benchmark against Pinnacle (the sharpest closing line in the world). You can place at any regulated bookmaker with ATP tennis markets. Pinnacle, Betfair Exchange, Bet365, and 1xBet recommended for best odds.
